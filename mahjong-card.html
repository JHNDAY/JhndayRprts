📖 How Scoring Works

x (exposure allowed):
You can call discards to complete sets and still win. The value shown (e.g. x25) is paid by each losing player to the winner, or doubled if won by self-draw on some house rules.
c (closed hand only):
You cannot call any discards — all tiles must come from the wall. You can only win by drawing the tile yourself. Closed hands pay more (c50, c60, c75) to reward the harder path.
