Key Insights & Recommendations

🎯 Exceptional Satisfaction

97% would recommend BOLLI to others with 95% satisfied with course quality. This indicates outstanding program delivery and member experience.

🔄 Hybrid Learning Success

Both modalities are valued: 55% prefer Zoom option, 36% prefer in-person. Continue supporting both to meet diverse member needs.

👥 Active Community Engagement

High participation rates: 65% attend Distinguished Speakers, 58% in Summer/Winter terms, 54% in Special Interest Groups.

🎓 Strong Leadership Pool

21% serve as Study Group Leaders who find the role rewarding. However, they need more technology support and prep time assistance.

🎨 Popular Subject Areas

Top interests: Art (49%), Literature (47%), History/Government (43%). Members request philosophy, contemporary topics, and hands-on courses.

🏢 Infrastructure Needs

Most frequent enhancement request: "larger and above ground meeting space." Current Turner Street facility limits in-person growth.

🔧 Technology Sophistication

All age groups comfortable with phones, internet, and Zoom. Members request advanced technology training (PowerPoint, AI).

🤝 Volunteer Potential

38% currently volunteer, with 94 additional members interested. Focus recruitment on recent members and 65-80 age group.

🚀 Future Opportunities

Expand course diversity • Enhance physical space • Increase social programming • Improve volunteer support • Advanced tech training
