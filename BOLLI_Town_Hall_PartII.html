Balance of Ambition vs. Reality

The town hall revealed a productive tension between the ambitious strategic plan and practical implementation concerns. While enthusiasm is high and ideas are plentiful, there's a clear need for:

  • Concrete prioritization and phasing
  • Task assignments and ownership
  • Resource assessment (volunteer capacity)
  • Quick wins to build momentum
  • Clear measurement of success

Community-First Approach

The strongest theme across all discussions was connection‚Äîmembers want to know each other better, help each other with technology, and contribute their skills. The directory issue, while complex, keeps surfacing as a fundamental need.

Volunteer Energy Untapped

Barb Thomas's suggestion to survey all members about their skills and availability represents a significant opportunity. With 500+ members, there's likely considerable expertise waiting to be engaged‚Äîthe challenge is matching needs to capabilities systematically.
