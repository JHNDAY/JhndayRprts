üéØ Reviewers' Core Objectives

Strategic Data Integrity

BOLLI's First Strategic Plan:

This analysis would inform major organizational decisions about space, technology, revenue, and community building. As Judy noted, "we're still collecting data until after September 10th" - making accuracy crucial for fundraising and organizational direction.

Balanced Member Representation:

Capturing all viewpoints, including the "sense of betrayal" some members felt about BOLLI's in-person class priorities, alongside positive feedback.

Quality Assurance

Precision & Accuracy:

Correcting details like participant counts and ensuring quotes were faithful to actual discussions. Marsha emphasized: "I took such careful notes that I can probably expand on this."

Appropriate Tone:

Replacing inflammatory language with accurate characterizations that reflected the respectful, collaborative nature of discussions.

Learning & Process

AI Collaboration Skills:

Judy explicitly stated she was participating "in part just to learn a little bit about" working with AI tools effectively - modeling for BOLLI's tech-curious community.

Replicable Framework:

Creating a model for analyzing future focus groups with consistent formatting and comparative analysis capabilities across different member subgroups.

Stakeholder Trust

Leadership Credibility:

Preparing trustworthy findings for strategic planning committee presentations while balancing completeness versus timeliness.

Member Confidence:

Ensuring analysis would be credible to BOLLI members, especially where focus group findings contradicted survey data about format preferences.

üí° Key Insights from the Process

The Interpretation Challenge:

As Marsha noted: "It's surprising to me how much interpretation this requires... the conversation is not quad." Raw transcripts miss nuance, emphasis, and social dynamics that human facilitators observed.

Quality Assurance as Gatekeeping:

The reviewers served as gatekeepers ensuring that AI's speed didn't compromise data integrity for BOLLI's first-ever strategic plan and potential fundraising efforts.

The Editorial Partnership:

John emphasized that without the editorial review process, "you're just going to miss the mark." AI provides patterns and suggestions, but human expertise ensures accuracy and appropriate emphasis.

Learning Through Collaboration:

Judy's approach of asking Claude to explain its reasoning ("Why did you characterize this as heated?") showed how the editing process itself became a learning opportunity about AI interpretation.
