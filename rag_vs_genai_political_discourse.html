RAG vs Generative AI — Current Events Reporting & Political Discourse
Approach A
Retrieval-Augmented Generation
Fetches live documents — wire dispatches, transcripts, vote records — then synthesizes a grounded response with traceable sources. Built for current events reporting.
Approach B
Generative AI (base model)
Draws entirely from patterns learned during training. Fluent, swift, and broad — suited for political discourse, ideology, and historical framing. The clock stopped at the training cutoff.

Neither approach is universally superior. RAG grounds political discourse in verifiable reality; base generative AI excels at synthesizing context, ideology, and historical pattern. Used together, they cover the full spectrum.
