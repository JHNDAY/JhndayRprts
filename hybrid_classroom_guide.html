🔍 Key Concept
The Zoom Room software acts as a single remote participant in your Zoom meeting, but sends up to 8 separate video feeds — one for each person detected in the Purple Room. In-person and remote participants can see and hear each other naturally.

1
🖥️ Turn On the Ceiling Projector

Tap the wall-mounted touchpad ("Welcome to BOLLI Purple Room: Touch Screen to Begin")

A Select Laptop/Mobile Source
B Choose Laptop HDMI — the cable plugged into the Mac Mini that connects to the ceiling projector
C Projector should show the Zoom Rooms display (Mac Mini desktop)
2
📱 Open the iPad

Retrieve the iPad from the locked cabinet (key: Robin or Dom).

A iPad passcode: bollii (two i's)
B Tap the Zoom app in the iPad dock; PIN to unlock: 123456
C Once in-meeting, the iPad shows the control panel below
📱 iPad — Zoom Room Control Panel (in-meeting)

The iPad control panel. Key buttons: Camera Control (to set Multi-Stream) and View (to set Gallery).

⚠️ Check WiFi
Ensure both iPad and Mac Mini are on Eduroam, not Brandeis Open.
3
🔗 Join the Zoom Meeting

On the iPad, enter the Meeting ID and passcode for the Host's Zoom session running on the laptop.

A Projector should then display video feeds from both the Host's laptop camera and the Nexigo camera
B To record: use the Host's laptop to record to the cloud
4
🎙️ Configure Audio

Important: Only ONE microphone and ONE speaker should be active in the room to prevent feedback echo.

Option A (preferred) Use the built-in room mic & speakers — plug USB-A + HDMI cables from the center front table into the Host's laptop, select them in Zoom. Then disable Nexigo mic & speaker in the iPad Zoom Room app.
Option B (fallback) Enable Nexigo's mic and speaker on the iPad app, and mute the Host laptop's mic and speaker.
🔇 Feedback Warning
Having two active microphones or speakers causes echo. Always disable one set before proceeding.
5
📷 Configure Camera & View on iPad

With the iPad control panel open, set the camera to Multi-Stream and the view to Gallery.

Camera Control → Select Multi-Stream

Tap Camera Control → change from Auto-Framing to Multi-Stream. Enables up to 8 individual participant feeds.

View → Select Gallery

Tap View → select Gallery so all in-room participants are visible to remote attendees.

A Tap Camera Control → switch from Auto-Framing to Multi-Stream
B Tap View → select Gallery
6
🏁 End of Class — Shutdown
A Tap Leave (red button) on the iPad control panel
B Close iPad cover; plug into USB-C power cable in cabinet; lock cabinet
C Leave the Mac Mini ON — do not shut it down
D Turn ceiling projector off via wall touchscreen
✅ Reminder
Mac Mini stays powered on between sessions. Only the iPad is stored and charged in the cabinet.

When Things Go Wrong
Troubleshooting & Re-Pairing
Projector shows "This room is not available" — Nexigo camera disconnected

This error means the Mac Mini can't find the Nexigo N3000 camera. Check that the camera USB cable is firmly plugged into the Mac Mini. You may need to re-pair the iPad and Mac Mini (see below).

Error screen visible from room — Nexigo disconnected

The Nexigo camera is visible mounted above the screen. Error message indicates it is disconnected.

Same error — note Exit & Sign Out buttons (bottom left)

Use the mouse/keyboard on top of the cabinet to click Exit on the Mac Mini before re-pairing.

iPad and Mac Mini are not paired / connected
To re-pair:
  1. Confirm both devices are on Eduroam WiFi
  2. Use mouse/keyboard on cabinet top to click Exit on the Mac Mini screen
  3. Exit the Zoom Room app on the iPad as well
  4. Start the Zoom Room app on the Mac Mini — it displays a 6-digit pairing code
  5. Start the Zoom Room app on the iPad and enter the code
  6. May take 1–2 attempts — this is normal
📖 Full Instructions
See Zoom's Quick Start Instructions for detailed pairing steps.
Only one video feed instead of multiple people
Camera is in Auto-Framing mode. On iPad → Camera Control → switch to Multi-Stream. Also set View → Gallery.
Echo / audio feedback in the room
Two mic/speaker sources are active. Choose one: either room built-in (via USB-A + HDMI cables from table) or Nexigo's — disable the other completely.
After a power outage or system restart
Both devices need to reconnect to Eduroam WiFi before re-pairing will succeed. Follow the full re-pairing procedure above.
