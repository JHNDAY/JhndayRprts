üêæ Important Reminders

  • Dog Parks (Off-Leash): Dogs must be licensed with current vaccinations. Keep dogs leashed when entering/exiting. Dogs must be under voice command at all times. Clean up after your dog.
  • Dog-Friendly Parks (Leashed): Dogs must be on a hand-held leash (6 feet maximum). Pick up after your pet and dispose of waste properly.
  • All Parks: Bring fresh water for your dog, especially on hot days. Watch for aggressive behavior and remove your dog if needed. Dogs not allowed in playgrounds or athletic fields.
  • Hours: Most parks open from 6-7 AM until sunset. Check individual park websites for specific hours.

For the most current information about park hours, rules, and amenities, visit Sarasota County Parks
