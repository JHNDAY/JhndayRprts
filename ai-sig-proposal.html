Hybrid Format
Every session available in-person and via Zoom — designed for full participation from either setting.
Collaborative at the Core
Sessions are about what participants bring — questions, findings, experiments — not lectures. Sharing is the curriculum.
Monthly Rhythm
Meets once a month, occasionally biweekly — each session sparks independent exploration that continues between gatherings.
What You Will Develop

AI Fundamentals

Key concepts for understanding how AI works — enough to use it confidently, question it wisely, and recognize its limits.

Digital Fluency

The practical literacy of prompting, refining, and directing AI — becoming a skilled navigator rather than a passive user.

Meaningful Output

Moving from query to creation — essays, visualizations, research summaries, course outlines — work that expresses your thinking.
