üéØ Speaker Tracking: What Remote Participants See
‚ñº
How Speaker Tracking Works (Without Individual Logins)

Audio-Based Detection: The system uses microphone arrays to detect which direction sound is coming from in the room, then adjusts the camera to focus on that area.

Key Limitation: Without individual logins, the system cannot identify WHO is speaking - it only knows WHERE the sound is coming from. No name tags or individual tiles for in-room speakers.

What Changes: The hardware determines whether remote participants see a static wide shot of everyone in the room, or an intelligent close-up that follows the active speaker.
