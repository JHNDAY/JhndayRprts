Strategic Summary

BOLLI stands at a critical crossroads with strong academic foundations and an engaged learning community, but faces significant financial sustainability challenges and infrastructure limitations. The organization must leverage its reputation for excellence while addressing cost pressures, expanding its reach to younger demographics, and adapting to post-COVID learning preferences. Success will require balancing the preservation of BOLLI's unique "community of learners" culture with necessary adaptations for long-term viability.
