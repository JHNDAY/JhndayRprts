Meeting Summary & Key Themes

Committee Composition

Diverse expertise including physics education, data science/AI, software development, technical support, and political science instruction. Team brings both technical depth and user-focused perspectives.

Core Tensions

  • Balancing implementation vs. advocacy roles
  • Technology for its own sake vs. practical effectiveness
  • Broad charter vs. achievable term goals
  • BOLLI-specific needs vs. cross-OLLI collaboration

Priority Areas

  • Zoom training and best practices
  • Accessibility across all platforms
  • Documentation and user guides
  • Knowledge sharing systems
  • AI tools for education

Philosophical Insight

Jerry's reminder that "just because the technology is available doesn't mean it's the most effective way" provides crucial grounding - the committee should evaluate technology adoption based on actual effectiveness, not novelty.

Emerging Opportunities

  • AI for course development (John Day's work)
  • Cross-OLLI knowledge sharing (Saul's vision)
  • Meeting transcription analysis
  • Comprehensive accessibility review
